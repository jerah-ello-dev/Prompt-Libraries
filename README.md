# Claude 6 best prompt to learn anything faster

## Prompt-LibrariesLearn Anything in 20 hours
prompt:
- i need to learn [topic] fast. Build me a 20-hour plan focused on the 20% that drives 80% of results. Break it into 10 two-hour sessions with the best resources and a 15-minute review at the end of each

## Create a one-page cheat sheet
prompt:
- summarize the key concepts of [topic] on a single page. use bullet points, diagrams, and examples so i can review it in 5 minutes

## Quiz me until i break
prompt:
- i just studied [topic]. give me 10 progressively harder questions to test my understanding. after each answer, grade me and explain what i missed.

## Build a learning ladder
prompt:
- Break [topic] into 5 levels of difficulty. show me how to go from level 1 (beginner) to level 5 (advanced) with a clear milestone at each step.

## Find the best learning resources
prompt:
- list the top 5 resources (books, videos, courses, or people) for learning [topic] fast, and explain why each is worth my time

## Use the Feyman technique
prompt:
- Explain [topic] to me in the simplest terms. Then have me re-explain it back. point out gaps, re-teach what i miss, and repeat until i can explain it clearly on my own.

## OPPORTUNITY MAPPING
- analyze my current role and skill set. identify 10 high-income AI automation opportunities i could monetize in my industry (consultin, internal systems or external services). prioritize ones with low startup time and high demand.

## MARKET VALIDATION
- for each opportunity, find companies currently paying for this problem. show pricing benchmarks, competitors, and where inefficiencies exist that AI could improve.

## PROFIT MODELING
- model a simple offer: if i charge Php2,000 - Php5,000 per client for this solution, how many clients do i need to rach Php10,000 per month? include delivery time, margins, and scalability.

## EXECUTION PLAN
- create a 30 day execution plan to land my first 2-3 clients while working a full-time job. focus on outreach, positioning, and fast proof-of-concept delivery.

## AUTOMATION & SCALE
- Design system using AI to automate delivery so i can fulfill clients in under 5 hours per week while maintaining quality.
